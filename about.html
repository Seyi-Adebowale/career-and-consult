ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

Career and Consult is an independent, Nigerian-owned company founded in 2022. We are a new breed of HR service provider with a technology-focused approach that places a premium on the quality of human capital, professionalism, and competence

Mission

To enable the discovery of opportunities by providing reliable HR solutions, connecting expertise and professionalism, providing our candidate with the necessary tool to meet our customers' needs.

Vision

  • Top ranking agency
  • Providing HR solutions that suits organizational structure.
  • Best talent source

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a range of HR solutions for businesses, including bulk hiring consultation, Permanent and Temporary Staffing, Onsite and Offsite Staffing. We work closely with companies to provide tailored HR services that meet their unique needs and help them find the right talent.

Our team is here to answer any questions you may have and provide information about our services. You can visit our "Contact" page for more details on how to reach us and to submit an inquiry form.

We specialize in a wide range of industries, including IT, healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and more. Our expertise extends to serving both large corporations and small to medium-sized enterprises across various sectors. If you have specific questions about our industry focus, feel free to contact our team for more details.
